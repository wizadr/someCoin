DevOps & support

DevOps aims to shorten the systems development life cycle and provide continuous delivery with high software quality. DevOps is complementary with Agile software development; several DevOps aspects came from the Agile methodology.

Other than it being a cross-functional combination of the terms and concepts for "development" and "operations," academics and practitioners have not developed a unique definition for the term "DevOps"

  • Automation of your installations

    • Setting up of technical environments
    • Deployment of the application

  • CI / CD Continuous Integration and Deployment

    • The basis of DevOps for continuous, reliable and fast delivery

  • Industrialization

    • Development standards
    • Scalability of developments
    • Configuration file

  • Capitalization

    • Knowledge is transmitted to your teams who gain in methodology
